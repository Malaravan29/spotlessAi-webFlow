Spotbot Xtreme - The Future of Autonomous Disinfection

Meet Spotbot Xtreme, the first-ever AI-powered, fully autonomous multiple-armed robot that's raising the bar for disinfection. Developed right here in the UK by Spotless AI, it harnesses cutting-edge UVC technology to cover every corner, zapping away 99.9999% of harmful pathogens in just 15 to 20 minutes. Perfect for busy environments, Spotbot Xtreme works independently, giving you a quick turnaround without skimping on safety.

01

The Superpower of Xtreme

Spotbot Xtreme is equipped with four UVC-powered arms designed for maximum flexibility and precision. With two arms positioned at the top and two at the bottom, they effortlessly reach tricky spots like under furniture or inside airplane luggage compartments. The lower arms can swivel 360 degrees, expand, and retract to tackle any angle, while the upper arms focus on tight spaces.

02

Smart Design for Efficient Disinfection

At just 42cm wide, Spotbot Xtreme can navigate narrow aisles, such as those found in airplanes, with ease. Its intelligent design ensures even light coverage and faster disinfection, making it an efficient and highly effective solution for maintaining cleanliness in challenging environments.

03

Long-Lasting Power

Spotbot Xtreme offers up to 6 hours of continuous operation on a single charge, making it ideal for extended use in busy settings. Its fast recharge time (6 hours) minimizes downtime, keeping disinfection operations running smoothly.

04

Trialed and Tested

Spotbot Xtreme has gone through serious testing processess across various locations in India, and it’s proven to be effective and reliable in real-world settings. These trials show it’s ready for action and meets the highest sanitation standards out there.

05

Proven Results

When it comes to performance, Spotbot Xtreme is impressive—it can reduce Colony Forming Units (CFUs) by a staggering 99.9999% and hits Log 6 in trial culture tests. This reduction indicates the robot’s effectiveness in eliminating pathogens and maintaining high standards of cleanliness and safety.

06

Disinfects Under 20 Minutes

Spotbot Xtreme can disinfect a typical 20 X 15 feet patient room in just 20 minutes. This rapid disinfection process ensures quick turnaround and thorough cleaning, making it ideal for high-demand environments such as hospitals, where prompt and effective sanitation is critical. Its efficiency also benefits busy clinics, as well as high-traffic areas such as public transportation hubs and large office spaces, where maintaining stringent cleanliness standards is essential

07

Advanced Environmental Recognition

Spotbot Xtreme employs multiple cameras to assess its surroundings, accurately recognizing the type of room and identifying areas where germs are likely to gather, such as high-touch surfaces. Its intelligent design allows it to manage shadowed spots effectively, adjusting its position to ensure comprehensive coverage.

08

Precision Disinfection Technology

Equipped with depth-sensing cameras, Spotbot Xtreme continuously measures the distance to surfaces, enabling it to adjust the UVC light intensity and disinfection time for targeted precision. Additionally, its arms operate in a parallel motion, ensuring optimal disinfection results on various shapes and objects.
