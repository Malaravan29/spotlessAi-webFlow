Spotbot Lite - Cost Effective AI-Driven Disinfection

Meet Spotbot Lite—your intelligent disinfection solution! Designed in the UK, this innovative robot offers 360-degree UVC coverage for thorough cleaning in any space. It’s cost-effective and efficient, combining smart technology with autonomous operation, so you can focus on what truly matters. Whether it’s a busy hospital or an office, Spotbot Lite makes hygiene easy and reliable!

01

Illuminate and Eliminate

Spotbot Lite is here to shine with its nine 4ft vertical UVC lights and eight horizontal lights—two on each side, two on top, and two underneath. Each lamp packs an impressive punch with 235 µW/cm³ of irradiance, ensuring every angle gets the disinfecting it deserves!

02

Disinfects under 30 mins

Spotbot Lite can disinfect a typical 20x15 feet patient room in under 30 minutes, it’s the quick and efficient solution you need when every minute counts, keeping your space safe and hygienic without the long wait.

03

Cost-effective design

Who says high-tech has to break the bank? Spotbot Lite is designed to be affordable without skimping on performance. It’s perfect for smaller clinics and educational institutions that want top-notch sanitation without hefty costs.

04

Long-Lasting

With a high-capacity battery that lasts up to 6 hours on a single charge, Spotbot Lite gives you the freedom to move around unencumbered by cords. Plus, a quick 6-hour charging time means it’s always ready to deliver effective disinfection when you need it!

05

Proven Efficacy

Spotbot Lite has undergone over 1,000 culture tests to demonstrate its effectiveness, achieving impressive Log 4 levels of disinfection. You can trust it to deliver reliable results every time.

06

Smart Environmental Awareness

Spotbot Lite uses multiple cameras to assess its surroundings and determine which room it’s in. Once it identifies the space, it detects various objects and spots that are likely germ hotspots—essentially, the areas that get touched the most.

07

Targeted Disinfection Approach

But it doesn’t stop there! Spotbot Lite also finds shadowed areas and ensures they’re disinfected by adjusting its approach, reaching around and sanitizing from the sides. With depth-sensing cameras, it continuously measures the distance to surfaces, allowing it to customize the UVC light dosage and intensity for effective, targeted disinfection every time.

08

An Eco-Friendly Choice

Spotbot Lite uses 256nm germicidal UV-C lamps to provide powerful, chemical-free disinfection. This tech is proven to wipe out pathogens without any harmful chemicals, making it an eco-friendly choice that’s good for both your health and the planet. With Spotbot Lite, you get thorough disinfection that cuts down on contamination, helping create a safer, healthier space for everyone.
