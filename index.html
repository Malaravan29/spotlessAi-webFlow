Here's Why Disinfection Matters More

Sure, scrubbing’s nice, but true disinfection is what really matters! Every year, around 300,000 folks in the UK deal with healthcare-associated infections (HAIs), racking up some serious health and financial costs.Traditional disinfection uses chemicals that can leave toxic residues. Enter UV disinfection: it zaps away up to 99.9999% of harmful germs—bacteria, viruses, and pathogens—without any harsh stuff. Fast and reducing human error, it’s perfect for hospitals, public spaces, and offices. UV disinfection guarantees thorough cleaning, boosts a healthier environment, and is eco-friendly.

WHY SPOTLESS AI?

Spotless AI is rewriting the rules with Spotbot Lite and Spotbot Xtreme, our AI-powered, autonomous UVC robots. These UK-designed geniuses make sure every inch of your space is pathogen-free, knocking out 99.9999% of harmful microbes in under 10 minutes - no sweat! Whether it’s healthcare, hospitality, or logistics, our bots saves you time, cuts down labor costs,  and help industries run smoother, safer, and way more efficiently.
